### Universal ATS Resume Tips (Beat Every System)

1. **Use standard section headers**: "Work Experience" not "My Journey"; "Education" not "Where I Learned"
2. **No tables, text boxes, columns, graphics, headers/footers**: ATS often cannot parse these
3. **Use .docx or clean PDF** (text-based, not image-based): Many ATS fail on image PDFs
4. **Mirror job description keywords exactly**: Same words, not synonyms (e.g., "JavaScript" not "JS")
5. **Spell out acronyms once**: "Search Engine Optimization (SEO)"
6. **No fancy fonts**: Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman, Helvetica
7. **Date format consistency**: MM/YYYY or Month YYYY — not just years alone
8. **Include full contact details**: phone, email, city/state at minimum
9. **Bullet points**: Simple hyphens or dots — not decorative characters
10. **Keywords in context**: Don't stuff — ATS increasingly check relevance

### Platform-Specific Tips

**Workday:** Create profile manually within portal · upload resume AND manually fill fields · work history must be entered separately · auto-population often incomplete

**Taleo:** Apply via company career site · keyword match is weighted · use exact title keywords from job description · avoid decorative formatting

**Greenhouse:** Often integrates with LinkedIn; keep LinkedIn current · skills-based sections valued · diversity questions are logged separately

**Lever:** Modern; GitHub/portfolio links are tracked · tailored cover letter counts more here than older ATS

**iCIMS:** Apply early — auto-ranking pushes later applicants down · keyword frequency matters · use all optional fields
