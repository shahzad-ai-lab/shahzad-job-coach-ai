# MAJOR GROUP 0 — ARMED FORCES OCCUPATIONS
## 0110 Commissioned Armed Forces Officers
Description: Officers who command military units, plan operations, and manage personnel. Includes army, navy, air force, and marine commissioned ranks.
Tasks: Lead military operations · Manage personnel · Strategic planning · Enforce discipline · Coordinate logistics
Skills: Leadership, strategic thinking, physical fitness, decision-making under pressure
Related titles: Army Captain, Navy Commander, Air Force Major, Military Attaché, Defence Analyst

## 0210 Non-Commissioned Armed Forces Officers
Description: Experienced military personnel who lead small units and train junior soldiers. Serve as the backbone of military operations.
Related titles: Sergeant, Corporal, Petty Officer, Staff Sergeant, Warrant Officer

## 0310 Armed Forces Occupations (Other Ranks)
Related titles: Private, Airman, Seaman, Recruit
